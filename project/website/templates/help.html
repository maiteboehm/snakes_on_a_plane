Help

General

Our navigation bar on top of the page helps you to navigate through our booking system.

  • Home: This will direct you to our home page where you have access to our seat reservation system.
  • Booking System: This will give you direct access to our seat reservation system.
  • Help: Since you are on our help page you might have already found this button, which leads you to this help page. In case you want to come back, just press this button in the navigation bar.
  • Logout: When you press this button, you will be logged out of the system and redirected to our login page.

How can I reserve a seat?

Navigate to our booking system page via the button in the navigation bar on top of the page or via the link on our home page. Afterwards, you can choose the flight, that you want to reserve a seat on, from the dropdown menu. Access the dropdown menu via pressing the button saying "Flights".

After choosing your flight, a chart with all seats on the flight will be displayed. Blue seats are still available and can be reserved by clicking on them. You will be asked to confirm your choice of seat in a popup. Please confirm your reservation by clicking "Reserve seat" in the popup. If you no longer want to reserve the seat you have clicked on, simply close the popup and you can continue to reserve other seats.

A seat, that has been reserved by you, will be displayed in green. If you want to cancel the reservation of one of those seats, simply click on the respective seat. A popup will ask you to confirm the cancellation by clicking "Cancel seat". If you decide not to cancel the seat, just close the popup and the respective seat will still be reserved by you.

Red seats are already taken by other users, therefore you will not be able to reserve them.

Once you have finished your reservation, click on the "Logout" button in the navigation bar on top of the page, to leave our websites.
